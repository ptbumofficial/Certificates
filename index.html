Welcome to Bedari Tech Pakistan

Baidari Tech Pakistan is a pioneering project of the Professionals of Tehreek-e-Baidari, working under the visionary leadership of Ustad Syed Jawad Naqvi. This initiative aims to empower youth by equipping them with essential skills in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Application Development, Graphic Designing, Video Editing, and other modern technologies.

We believe that an ideologically grounded (Nazriyati) youth must also be technologically empowered to effectively contribute to shaping the future. Baidari Tech Pakistan serves as a bridge between ideology and innovation, enabling young individuals to serve society with both vision and technical expertise.
